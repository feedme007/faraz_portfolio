About IEEE CS SYP Congress

The IEEE CS SYP Global Congress is a highly innovative initiative to help the student members learn about cutting-edge technology from the most skilled professionals in the industry. From cultivating professional relationships to networking with fellow delegates, IEEE CS SYP Congress is here to bring you highly tech-driven webinars from the industry’s top-tier speakers that polish your professional skills and help you learn about the upcoming technology. 

Why you need to participate?

IEEE CS SYP Global Congress brings you a chance to connect, advance, and lead. Participate in our congress and get a chance to

  • Improve your professional skills 
  • Learn about the concepts of emerging tech in the fields of IoT, AI/ML, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, UI/UX Research & Design, and much more.
  • Unlimited networking opportunities
  • Participate in pre-congress events and competitions to exciting win prizes and awards
  • Accelerate your professional growth
