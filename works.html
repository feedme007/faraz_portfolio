Social Media Crowdsourcing for Traffic Congestion Management

Created a syntactical geocoder to map congestion-related tweets to routes rather than isolated points. Developed an ontology and applied network centrality measures to optimize urban traffic flow, resulting in more accurate traffic predictions.
This project won 3rd prize at a national-level competition organized by the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, and the paper is currently under review at IJGIS (Taylor & Francis).

  • Developed a geocoding framework in Python
  • Integrated network analysis for urban traffic optimization
  • Delivered actionable insights for improved traffic management

  • Recommended optimized traffic management strategies

Optimized Feature Selection Framework for Efficient Heart Sound Classification

Developed an innovative framework for classifying heart sounds to aid early cardiovascular disease diagnosis. Employed MFCCs for feature extraction and a genetic algorithm for feature selection, achieving high classification accuracy.
I won the Best Presenter Award at "Advancing Technology for Sustainable Development" for this project, and the paper has been published in IEEE.

  • Utilized MFCCs for effective feature extraction
  • Applied genetic algorithms for optimal feature selection
  • Achieved impressive accuracy in binary and multiclass scenarios
